Background

Donna West owns a child care center. The mission of her business is to educate and entertain children while keeping them safe. Child care centers are a unique business as they must meet various different regulations. It can be difficult to track all of the needed information on paper as this would require a significant amount of data to organize and store. Our client would like an application that encompasses the major aspects of her business.

Scope

Donna's Daycare application will allow her to track and manage information on the children she is caring for, maintain a constant communication portal with parents, and store business-related accounting information.

Proposed Solution

Donna's daycare application will be primarily used as a mobile application that consists of three major modules:

  • Accounting Module:
    • Maintain a chart of accounts to hold business expenses.
    • A billing system to:
      • Track parent payments
      • Have the ability to send billing reminders to parents.
  • Child Management Module:
    • Maintain a record for each child containing child-related information such as:
      • Child's name
      • Child's DOB
      • Emergency contacts
      • Guardians approved for child pickup
    • Utilize a barcode reader to check children in and out of the daycare.
  • Annoucements Module:
    • Provide the ability to send messages to all parents
    • Provide the ability to communicate with individual parents

Highlights

  • From maintaining accounting information to tracking children, this application will allow our client to manage most major aspects of her business.
